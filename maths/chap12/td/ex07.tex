\part{Exercice 7}

Soit $f: \left( \Q, + \right) \to \left( \Q_*, \times  \right)$ un isomorphisme.\\

On pose  \[
	\begin{cases}
		a = f^{-1}(2) \in \Q\\
		b = \frac{a}{2} \in \Q
	\end{cases}
\] Domme $a = 2b$, on a $2 = f(a) = f(b + b) = f(b)\times f(b) = f(b)^2$\\
Donc, $f(b) = \pm \sqrt{2}$. Or, $f(b) \in \Q_*$.
$\lightning$
