				We define the sequence $(w_n)_{n\in\N}$ by  \[
					\forall n \in \N, w_n = au_n + bv_n
				\]
				Let $n\in \N$.
				\begin{align*}
					w_{n+2} &= au_{n+2} + bv_{n+2}\\
					&= 2au_{n+1} + 3au_n + 2bv_{n+1} + 3bv_n \\
					&= 2w_{n+1} + 3w_n\\
				\end{align*}
				So, $F_1$ is a subspace of $E$
		\end{itemize}
